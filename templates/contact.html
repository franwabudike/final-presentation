Frequently Asked Questions

How do you choose playlists?

Our playlists are hand-curated by our team and also include popular community-contributed playlists that align with specific study moods. We use a combination of expert selection and community feedback to ensure quality.

Can I suggest new moods?

Absolutely! We are always open to new ideas. You can use the contact form to send us your suggestions for new moods or features you'd like to see on VibeRoom.

Is VibeRoom free to use?

Yes, VibeRoom is completely free to use. You only need to have an account with YouTube Music or Spotify to access and listen to the playlists.

Do you support other platforms?

Currently, we support YouTube Music and Spotify. We are actively exploring the possibility of integrating with other platforms in the future based on user demand.
